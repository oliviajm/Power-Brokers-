About Garry's List

Garry's List is a civic accountability publication covering California politics, public spending, and the institutions that shape everyday life in the state. We follow the money, track the votes, and name the people responsible when government fails the public it serves.

We believe Californians deserve to know where their tax dollars go, why outcomes keep getting worse despite record budgets, and who is blocking reform. Our investigations, data tools, and reporting are built to make that information accessible, verifiable, and impossible to ignore.

Founded by Garry Tan. Based in San Francisco.
