About Garry's List

Garry's List is a civic accountability organization covering California politics, public spending, and the institutions that shape everyday life in the state. We follow the money, track the votes, and name the people responsible when government fails the public it serves.

Our work spans investigative reporting, interactive civic data, and political action, each housed under its own home. This hub connects them all so Californians can find the information that affects them, verify it, and act on it.

Founded by Garry Tan. Based in San Francisco.
